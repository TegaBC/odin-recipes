Fried Chicken

Description: Chicken covered in breadcrumbs, and fried in oil. A family favourite.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breast
  • Seasoning of your choice
  • 100ml of egg whites or 2-3 eggs
  • breadcrumbs

Steps:

  1. Cover chicken in egg whites or regular eggs
  2. Add seasoning of your choice to the chicken
  3. Then toss your chicken in a bowl with breadcrumbs
  4. Repeat for all chicken breasts and make sure they are covered
  5. Fry in a pan or use the air fryer for no oil!
