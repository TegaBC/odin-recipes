Minced Turkey Wrap

Description: A tortilla wrap filled with fresh minced turkey, a leaner alternative to beef!

Ingredients:

  • Tortilla Wrap
  • Seasoning of your choice
  • Minced/Diced Turkey

Steps:

  1. Add oil to a pan and fry the minced turkey.
  2. Add seasoning to the mince whilst it is in the pan.
  3. Prepare tortilla for plating.
  4. Cook mince until it is brown.
  5. Put mince on the tortilla
  6. Add sauces and extras and then finally wrap it up.
