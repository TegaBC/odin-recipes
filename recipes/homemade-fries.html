Home Made Fries

Description: Home made fries, made using rustic potatos!

Ingredients:

  • Potatos
  • Seasoning of your choice
  • Oil (Not needed if you use an air fryer.)

Steps:

  1. Cut potatos into fry shapes
  2. Add seasoning to the fries (we recommend: Peri Peri seasoning)
  3. Fry in oil or fry in the air fryer.
  4. Server with sauces of your choice!
